Adrift

PURCHASE

"Real Life? Never Heard Of That Server."


Adrift Vr is an immersive VR experience designed through Unity. This Vr experience is created to empathize on the connection between children and parents. Adrift Vr allow the audience the be fully immersed into the children’s dream through the first person simulation.


Due to our fast paced society, parents often find themselves busy with work and were not able to spend time to connect with their children. Through our project, we want to create a strong emotional bridge for adults to experience the loneliness and the sorrows that children felt. The user will experience the little girl’s feelings in the first person perspective. Ultimately, after this experience, we hope more parents will pay more attention to their children’s feeling and thoughts, as well as thinking about the potential impact of not spending enough time with children.


In this game, the participant should experience an emotional journey through a simulation of children's dream. In order to achieve this emotional ride, we implemented both sensory and imaginative immersion. Emotions are conveyed through visual details and sound effects. For example, within the first scene, the user should feel fear and loneliness with the grayish dark lighting, the creepy soundtrack, and the spider that is crawling around.
