Another Me

PURCHASE

"Real Life? Never Heard Of That Server."


Another Me is a 2D platformer game set in a post-apocalypse world where the human race is extinct, There are only the android robots that was created by the human left on the planet. The main protagonist Serena set out on a journey to find her true self and her background.


Protagonist has a drug problem and is sent to rehab but unfortunately was redirected to a asylum for to a and friends were invited to a party, not knowingly, they were drugged and captured inside different rooms. The whole building are completely sealed. At the very beginning, the rooms are locked, player are asked to go through the entire room to figure out clues to escape from the room. While doing so, characters all receives a message about “every hour one of you guys are going to die in a horrible way”. After escaping the room, the protagonist hears a loud scream, and when he reached the room, he found out one of his best friend has been stabbed, and bleed out to death. His other friend who also escaped their room heard the scream too, reached to room a few minutes late and think that protagonist is the criminal.
