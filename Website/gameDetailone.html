Adventure Of Blobby

PURCHASE

"Real Life? Never Heard Of That Server."


Adventure of Blobby is a 2D platformer created in Unity on the PC and mobile platform.The game is about a lost blob trying its hardest to navigate through the dangerous forest, solving multiple puzzles and defeat spooky monsters to find and reach its family.


Mechanics of the game are mainly taught in the first tutorial level, as the player navigate through the level, player is able to visually see signs that teach them what to do in certain situation. The signs were then removed from the game after the first level. Map System - Key[M]; Map System allows the player to zoom out the camera and see the whole map in a further distance. This allows easier navigation throughout the level. This system runs on a cooldown system, this prevent the player from staying and playing within the map, instead of playing in the actual game.
